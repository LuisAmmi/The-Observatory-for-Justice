The magazine

The Observer for Justice dedicates a special number to 20 years of chronic police abuse. From the 27th summit of G8 Genoa, marked by violent clashes between anti-globalisation protesters and police, to the "Black Lives Matter" movement, boomed after the the murder of George Flyod and other episodes of to racial hatred perpetuated by white officer.

The issue

This time, the Observer main issue of his number: each issue will explore a specific topic, covering it from different perspectives. We always try to offer the widest picture possible. We also offer additional tools that the reader can use to connect entities inside the texts and to further expand his or her knowledge.

The project

Our magazine started as a University project within the University of Bologna course in Information Modelling and Web Technology held by Professor Fabio Vitali in the academic year 2019-2020. Our main aim was to explore the different ways people form the past (and the future) styled texts, approaching this as an expression of the taste of that period.
